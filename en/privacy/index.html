Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 11, 2026

At We Code Studio ("we", "our", "us"), we respect your privacy and are committed to protecting the personal information you share with us. This policy explains what we collect, how we use it, and how we keep it protected.

1. Information we collect

We only collect information you voluntarily provide through our contact form, including:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Phone or WhatsApp number (optional)
  • Service interest
  • Company type, estimated budget and timeline
  • Project goals and description

2. How we use your information

We use this information exclusively to:

  • Respond to your inquiry or quotation request
  • Understand project scope and fit
  • Send a proposal when requested

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for advertising purposes.

3. Third-party services

Our contact form uses Formspree to process submissions. Submitted data is sent to our email and may be temporarily stored by Formspree as part of their service.

We host this website on Cloudflare Pages, which may process technical request data for security and delivery.

We use Google Fonts for typography, which may create requests to Google servers.

4. Analytics and tracking

We use privacy-friendly web analytics to measure high-level site performance and conversion events such as CTA clicks and form submissions. We do not use invasive ad tracking pixels or build personal behavior profiles. Analytics data is aggregated and used only to improve service quality and user experience.

5. Security

We use standard security practices including HTTPS/TLS, security headers, and anti-spam protections in forms.

6. Your rights

You may request access, correction, or deletion of your personal data at any time by contacting us at info@wecodestudio.com.

7. Data retention

We keep contact data only as long as needed to respond and complete the quotation process. If no project moves forward, we aim to remove your data within 90 days.

8. Policy updates

We may update this policy occasionally. The "last updated" date indicates the current version.
